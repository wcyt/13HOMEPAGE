Lake Biwa

Summary

position:Shiga Prefecture

surface area:670km2

Feature: Largest area and the largest water storage capacity in Japan

There are more than 10 lake bathing spots in Lake Biwa. Where the water is not sticky when it dries, where there is plenty of nature, and where there are facilities for camping, making it a different kind of fun than swimming. Also, since there are nowaves, even beginners can enjoy marine sports here.

Environment

It is a huge lake, occupying one sixth of the area of Shiga Prefecture. The lake is the 13th in the world to be created, and there are only about 20 ancient lakes in the world (has a history of more than one million years).

Features

The lake is divided into North and South lakes by the Lake Biwa Bridge, which spans the narrowest part of the landform. It is the largest and deepest lake in the Japan, and most of its water is stored in North Lake. The most deep is a little north of the center of the North Lake and the deepest point is 103 meters.
