Ginzan Onsen
Environment
Spring which makes the mountains of the lingering snow drunk with the fragrance of spring little by little, and is dyed in the color of the mountain cherry tree. The wild vegetables which appear just as I was waiting for are excellent flavor.
The chirping of birds, the sound of waterfalls and mountain streams. The sound of biting frogs and cool watermelons. You can find a cool summer in the walking course after taking a hot bath.
A stroll while viewing the colorful autumn leaves is exceptional. You can satisfy your appetite with mushroom dishes and enjoy the autumn outing to your heart's content.
The light from the gas lamps that light up the silver world is even more romantic. A bath and a hot potluck awaits your chilled body.
