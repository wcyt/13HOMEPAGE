kamuishu Island
There is a small island formed by a lava dome called Kamuish Island.It is surrounded by cliffs and is 110m long, 40m short and 30m high.The island is surrounded by cliffs and the vegetation is mainly royal azaleas and lingonberries,with few ferns and grasses. There are few ferns and ferns.Even the lake surface is inaccessible and civilians are not allowed to land on the island.

Environment

It was formed when water accumulated in a hollow created by a massive eruption about 7,000 years ago. A very poorly nourished lake with very few nutrients. The water quality is clean because it is not connected to the river and the rain is filtered through the soil and flows into the river. The water in Lake Mashu faithfully reflects the effects of air pollution and is therefore a target for monitoring to learn about environmental changes on the planet.

Features

In 1930, Lake Baikal recorded 41.6 meters, eclipsing Lake Baikal (40.5 meters) as the clearest lake in the world. The next survey in 1952, however, the clarity dropped to 29 meters, and it continued to deteriorate to 17.2 meters in 2015. Currently, it has recovered to 28.0 meters, which ranks second in the world and first in the country.
