Summary
Mt.Fuji
Fuji is an active volcano that straddles the border between Shizuoka and Yamanashi prefectures. With an elevation of 3,776 meters, its elegant appearance is widely known as a symbol of Japan even outside of Japan. The mountain has been the subject of many artistic works, and has been used as a symbol of Japan. The geology of the climate and strata and other geological and social influences are significant.
Mt.Osore
Mt.osore is an active volcano located in the center of the Shimokita Peninsula (Aomori Prefecture). Mt.Usori is an active volcano located in the center of the Shimokita Peninsula (Aomori Prefecture, Japan), and is home to the Osorezan Bodhiji Temple, one of the three major sacred sites in Japan, on the shore of Lake Usori, a caldera lake. Mt osore is distinct from the so-called Osorezan sacred mountain range, as it occupies the northern part of the Shimokita Peninsula.
Environment
Monthly average temperature at Mt.Fuji
Even in August, the warmest month, the average temperature at the summit is only 6 degrees Celsius, and in March, April, May, and June, the summit has one of the top 10 deepest snowfalls. The lowest temperature in recorded history was -38.0°C on February 27, 1981, and there have been a few days in the past when the maximum temperature was below -30°C. A day with a maximum temperature of less than -30°C has never been seen in Hokkaido.

Specialty
Chiffon Fuji
Chiffon Fuji is a chiffon cake shop located in Fujiyoshida City. The fluffy and moist chiffon dough is baked and covered with powdered sugar to represent snow. In 2015, it won the prefecture award at THE JAPAN GIFT AWARDS.
Cold water from Mt.Osore
On the Osorezan Highway (Aomori Prefectural Road 4), which leads from Mutsu City to the Osorezan Sacred Ground, there is a spring of cold water called "Hiyamizu" on the way.It is said to rejuvenate you if you drink it, but since it is unpasteurized water without filtration or sterilization, it is better to boil it once before drinking it.
Festival
horseback archery festival
Yabusame (horseback archery) is a martial art of shooting a target from a running horse. According to the 1577 "Fuji-Omiya Shrine Shrine Shrine Book", it is said to have started with the dedication of horseback archery to the shrine to pray for longevity of war fortune and peace under heaven. This ritual has a tradition of over 800 years, which is described in the 1650 "Fuji Hongu Nenrye no Zaidan".
Mt.Osore Festival
Mt.Osore hosts two major festivals a year, one in summer and one in autumn. Many visitors from all over the country visit the summer festival in Mount Osorezan. It is said that "If you pray to Jizo on the festival day, you will be saved from the suffering of the deceased. This is the reason why the festival is so crowded with people who have made a pilgrimage in autumn.
